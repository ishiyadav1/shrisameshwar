About Ashram

Ashram is a sacred project initiated by a group of spiritual seekers with the vision of creating a peaceful sanctuary in the holy city of Haridwar, where individuals from all walks of life can come to meditate, study spiritual texts, and connect with like-minded souls.

Located near the banks of the Ganges, our ashram will serve as a spiritual retreat center offering accommodation, meditation halls, a wisdom library, and spaces for satsang and spiritual discourse.

"Just as the Ganges flows through Haridwar, may the divine grace flow through this sacred space, touching all who enter with peace and wisdom."

Why Haridwar?

Haridwar, meaning "Gateway to God," is one of the seven holiest places in Hinduism, where the Ganges leaves the mountains and enters the plains.

Every twelve years, Haridwar hosts the Kumbh Mela, the largest peaceful gathering in the world, where millions come to bathe in the sacred river.

An ashram in Haridwar provides the perfect environment for spiritual growth, with the divine energy of the Ganges and the presence of countless saints and seekers.
